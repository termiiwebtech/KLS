About us

Kingdom Life Seminar

THIS IS WHO WE ARE

Kingdom Life Seminar is a non denominational fellowship of believers with headquarters in Lagos Nigeria under the oversight of Dr Okey Onuzo, President Lifelink Worldwide Ministries.

MEETING TIMES & LOCATION

In Lagos: Every Saturday except for the last Saturday of the month at 7am
Venue: Suite 1, Canaan Plaza, Lekki-Epe Expressway, by Marwa Bus Stop/Mobolaji Johnson Estate, Lagos (Off the 2nd Roundabout) Lekki/Ajah Expresway.

PRAYER MEETING: Every Friday at 6pm
Venue: 1B, Kayode Otitoju Street, Off Admiralty Way, Lekki Phase 1, Lagos

In Calabar: Second Thursday of the month at 6pm
Venue: 164/165 Murtala Mohammed Way. Same premises as The Women and Children’s Hospital.

In Abuja: First Monday of the Month at 6pm
Venue: Eden Plaza – The Father’s Church Banex – Gwarinpa Bypass at Jahi Opposite Next Cash & Carry Supermarket

OUR MISSION & VISION
Mission

KLS was born out of a deep desire to bring the children of God into the victory of Christ in their daily living. The passion is to see men and women who profess the name of Christ walk in the footprints of Jesus with power.

Vision

To raise a community of believers in Christ who deeply love their God and as a result have been empowered to love their fellow men and women.
